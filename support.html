Smile255 is always trying to raise money to be able to send more Tanzanian youth players to more tournaments. Sending a player to a tournament in a neighboring country costs roughly 150USD when factoring in travel, food, lodging, tournament fees, and government fees. In addition to sending players to tournaments, normal expenses include cones, discs, bibs, and water for training. If you would like to support Smile255 in any way, please reach out to smile255@gmail.com.


Many thanks for the continued support from friends of Smile255 such as Collin Spurway, Ueli Listener, Jenny Tiberio, Simon Hurmony, and Elizabeth Grace Nowlin

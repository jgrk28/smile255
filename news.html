Swahili International Beach Club Tournament

In November of 2022, Smile255 will host the first ever Tanzanian international beach ultimate frisbee tournament in Mwanza. This tournament was organized by Mwinyi Fantastic with the goal of inspiring players from all over Tanzania. Mwinyi hopes to have teams attend from Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, and beyond bringing a level of play to Tanzania that has never been seen before. Since the goal of this tournament is to promote the future of the sport in Tanzania and East Africa, there will be insentives such as no tournament fee for one U20 female player per team.
